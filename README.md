# NEXUS Sales OS — Autonomous Revenue Intelligence System

NEXUS Sales OS is a production-ready, AI-powered Sales & Revenue Intelligence platform. It goes beyond standard analytics by actively tracking deals, detecting risks, generating recovery strategies, and automating personalized outreach using a coordinated multi-agent AI system.

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Multi-Agent Architecture**: 10 specialized AI agents working together to provide comprehensive revenue intelligence.
- **Real-Time Risk Detection**: Proactively identifies bottlenecks, silent deals, and at-risk revenue.
- **Autonomous Outreach**: Generates contextually aware, personalized 5-touch sequences for emails, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn.
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Automatically generates dynamic battlecards and objection handlers.
- **Retention Risk Analysis**: Predicts customer churn and generates actionable intervention plans.
- **Revenue Impact Calculation**: Quantifies the ROI of AI interventions by projecting revenue saved.
- **Full Audit Trail**: Transparent logging of every AI decision, input, and reasoning.
- **Stunning UI**: Modern, dark-themed Single Page Application (SPA) with glassmorphism, dynamic animations, and real-time AI typing effects.

## 🧠 The 10 AI Agents

1. **Signal Agent**: Analyzes engagement signals (no reply times, meeting cancellations).
2. **Pipeline Intelligence Agent**: Detects stage delays, bottlenecks, and overall revenue at risk.
3. **Deal Intelligence Agent**: Calculates a precise risk score (0-100) and risk factors for individual deals.
4. **Prospecting Agent**: Evaluates leads using ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) scoring and generates initial sequences.
5. **Strategy Agent**: Formulates optimal action plans (Executive Escalation, Proactive Engagement, etc.) based on deal context.
6. **Outreach Agent**: Generates tailored messages for specific personas and platforms.
7. **Retention Agent**: Monitors customer health, predicts churn probability, and plans interventions.
8. **Competitive Agent**: Prepares battlecards and positions your product against known competitors.
9. **Revenue Impact Agent**: Calculates the specific monetary value (ROI) of running the AI pipelines.
10. **Orchestrator Agent**: Coordinates the entire workflow seamlessly across all specialized agents.

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- **Backend**: Python 3, FastAPI, Pydantic, Uvicorn
- **Frontend**: Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Properties, Flexbox/Grid, Animations), JavaScript (ES6 Modules, Fetch API)
- **Data Store**: In-memory Python DataStore (pre-seeded with demo data)

## 🛠️ Usage

### Running an AI Analysis
1. Navigate to the **Deals Intelligence** tab from the sidebar.
2. Click the `🤖 Analyze` button next to any deal (High, Medium, or Low risk).
3. Watch the **Orchestrator Agent** sequentially trigger the analysis pipeline.
4. View the real-time AI reasoning, risk breakdowns, and generated outreach messages.

### Exploring the Platform
- **Dashboard**: Get a bird's-eye view of your Pipeline Health, Revenue at Risk, and Stage Distributions.
- **Prospecting**: Review ICP scores and multi-touch sequences for fresh leads.
- **Retention**: Monitor existing customers for churn indicators and drop-offs.
- **Competitive Intel**: View automatically generated battlecards against target competitors.
- **Audit Logs**: Trace exactly what every AI agent analysed and decided, ensuring 100% transparency.
